About Me

My introduction

Hi! I'm April, 20 years Old from the town of Santa Maria, Pangasinan. From childhood, creativity resonated within my home, nurtured by the wisdom of my beloved Grandmother. My dreams of design and innovation have blossomed into a life of endless creativity. In my haven, every idea becomes a captivating design, weaving dreams into reality. A journey of exploration, experience, and an unyielding desire to bring visions to life. As a leader in our church's IT committee, I discovered the powerful fusion of technology and design. Now, a vital member of Pangasinan State University's Design Committee, I am shaping the future, where creativity meets academic excellence. Join me on this captivating journey, where dreams transform into living designs. Let's paint the world with innovation and artistic brilliance.
